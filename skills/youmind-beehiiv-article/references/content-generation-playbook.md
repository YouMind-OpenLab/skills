# Beehiiv Content Generation Playbook

1. Lock the angle in one sentence.
2. Draft a title that feels like a newsletter subject line, not a blog keyword dump.
3. Open with stakes or payoff immediately.
4. Break the body into 3-5 sections with scannable subheads.
5. End with one clear takeaway or next step.
6. Produce clean HTML preview and ask for publish intent if unclear.
