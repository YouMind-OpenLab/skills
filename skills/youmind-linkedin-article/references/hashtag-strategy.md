# LinkedIn Hashtag Strategy

## Rules

1. **3–5 hashtags per post.** Less looks incomplete, more looks spammy.
2. **Mix broad + niche:** 1–2 broad (>100K followers) + 2–3 niche (<50K followers)
3. **Place at the end** of the post, separated by a line break.
4. **No spaces in hashtags.** Use CamelCase for readability: #ArtificialIntelligence, not #artificialintelligence
5. **Research before using.** Click the hashtag to check follower count and recent posts.

## Hashtag Tiers

### Tier 1: Broad (>500K followers) — use 1
High reach, high competition. Your post appears briefly then gets buried.
- #Innovation #Leadership #Technology #Marketing #Entrepreneurship
- #AI #MachineLearning #DataScience #CloudComputing

### Tier 2: Industry (50K–500K) — use 1–2
Good balance of reach and competition.
- #SaaS #ProductManagement #StartupLife #RemoteWork #AgileMethodology
- #GenerativeAI #NLP #ComputerVision #MLOps #DevOps

### Tier 3: Niche (<50K) — use 1–2
Lower reach but higher engagement and relevance.
- #AIAgents #LLMOps #TechLeadership #FounderLife #BuildInPublic
- #RAG #PromptEngineering #VectorDatabase #AIProductivity
