# LinkedIn Content Frameworks

## Post vs Article Decision Tree

```
Content length > 1500 chars?
├── Yes → Article
└── No
    ├── Deep analysis / multi-section? → Article
    ├── Personal story + one insight? → Post
    ├── Quick tip / observation? → Post
    └── Listicle (>7 items)? → Article
```

## Post Frameworks

### 1. Hook → Story → Insight → CTA
The most reliable LinkedIn post format.
- **Hook (1–2 lines):** Bold statement or surprising fact
- **Story (3–5 paragraphs):** Personal experience with concrete details
- **Insight (1–2 paragraphs):** The lesson or takeaway
- **CTA (1 line):** Question for engagement

### 2. Hook → List → Takeaway
Fast to read, high save rate.
- **Hook:** "X things I learned from Y"
- **List:** 3–7 items, each 1–2 sentences
- **Takeaway:** The meta-lesson or invitation to share more

### 3. Contrarian Take
High risk, high reward. Drives comments.
- **Hook:** "Everyone says X. I disagree."
- **Evidence:** 2–3 concrete counter-examples
- **Nuance:** Acknowledge when the conventional wisdom IS right
- **Invitation:** "Change my mind" or "What's your take?"

### 4. Before/After Transformation
Shows growth, very relatable.
- **Before:** "X years ago, I believed..."
- **Turning point:** The event/insight that changed everything
- **After:** "Now I know that..."
- **Lesson:** What the reader can apply

## Article Frameworks

### 1. Problem → Analysis → Solution
Classic thought leadership format.
- **Intro:** Define the problem with data/examples
- **Analysis:** Why existing solutions fall short (2–3 sections)
- **Solution:** Your framework or approach
- **Application:** How to implement it
- **CTA:** Invite discussion

### 2. Narrative → Framework → Application
Story-driven analysis.
- **Story:** Compelling narrative that illustrates the topic
- **Framework:** Extract the principles behind the story
- **Application:** How readers can use these principles
- **Examples:** 2–3 additional cases

### 3. Trend → Deep Dive → Predictions
Industry analysis format.
- **Trend identification:** What's happening and why it matters
- **Deep dive:** Data, examples, expert perspectives
- **Implications:** What this means for the industry
- **Predictions:** Where this is heading
- **Action items:** What to do about it now
